# Version Roadmap

## Current Version: v1.0.0

The initial release focused on core gameplay: movie production pipeline, talent management, simulation engine, and basic studio economics.

---

## Planned Releases

### v1.1.0 — Studio Expansion
**Target: Q3 2026**

- [ ] **Awards System** — Full awards season simulation with nominations, red carpet events, and trophy collection
- [ ] **Franchise Management** — Enhanced franchise mechanics with cross-movie bonuses and brand building
- [ ] **Studio Design** — Customizable studio headquarters with visual upgrades
- [ ] **Enhanced Rival AI** — More sophisticated rival studio decision-making with adaptive strategies

### v1.2.0 — Industry Depth
**Target: Q4 2026**

- [ ] **Genre Trends & Events** — Dynamic industry trends that shift audience preferences
- [ ] **Seasonal Events** — Holiday box office seasons, summer blockbuster window, awards season
- [ ] **Crew Specialization** — Expanded crew roles with unique abilities and synergies
- [ ] **Marketing Revamp** — More granular marketing campaigns with targeted demographics
- [ ] **Multiplayer Mode** — Basic multiplayer: compare stats with friends, shared leaderboards

### v2.0.0 — Cinematic Universe
**Target: Q1 2027**

- [ ] **AI Studios** — Fully autonomous AI studios with unique personalities and long-term strategies
- [ ] **Sequels & Franchises** — Deep franchise mechanics with shared universes and crossover events
- [ ] **Expanded Production** — Animated films, documentaries, international co-productions
- [ ] **Music & Soundtrack** — Commission original scores, soundtrack sales
- [ ] **Talent Agency** — Represent and manage external talent for other studios

### v2.1.0 — Global Markets
**Target: Q2 2027**

- [ ] **Regional Markets** — Distinct box office performance across regions (North America, Europe, Asia, etc.)
- [ ] **Film Festivals** — Cannes, Sundance, TIFF with premiere rights and awards
- [ ] **International Co-Productions** — Partner with foreign studios for tax incentives and market access
- [ ] **Localization** — Dubbing, subtitling, cultural adaptation for international releases

### v2.2.0 — Studio Legends
**Target: Q3 2027**

- [ ] **Hall of Fame** — Legacy system tracking historic achievements across playthroughs
- [ ] **Achievement System** — 100+ achievements with rewards and unlocks
- [ ] **New Game Plus** — Carry over certain upgrades and reputation to new playthroughs
- [ ] **Challenge Scenarios** — Pre-defined starting conditions with unique objectives

### v3.0.0 — Connected Universe
**Target: 2028**

- [ ] **Multiplayer Economy** — Shared marketplace where players trade talent and scripts
- [ ] **Co-op Productions** — Joint movie productions between players
- [ ] **Studio Alliances** — Form alliances, share resources, dominate the box office together
- [ ] **Global Leaderboards** — Real-time competitive rankings
- [ ] **Expansion Packs** — Major content packs (Horror Studio, Animation House, Indie Darling)

---

## Release Philosophy

- **Minor versions** (v1.1, v1.2) — Add features without breaking existing saves
- **Major versions** (v2.0, v3.0) — May include breaking changes requiring new save games
- **Patch releases** (v1.0.1) — Bug fixes and small improvements only

## How to Contribute

Check the `good first issue` label on GitHub for beginner-friendly tasks aligned with the roadmap. For larger features, please open a discussion first to coordinate with maintainers.
